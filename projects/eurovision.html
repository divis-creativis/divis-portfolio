Eurovision

Eurovision is more than a music contest – it’s a celebration of diversity, creativity and unity. For this project we created a suite of graphics and animations that capture the energy of the stage and the camaraderie between countries. Bold colours, playful shapes and rhythmical motion evoke the exuberance of the event.

Our deliverables included broadcast packaging, social media assets and merchandise designs, all tailored to resonate with a global audience. The identity underscores the core Eurovision values: fun, inclusivity and inspiration.
