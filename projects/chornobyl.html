Chornobyl

Client – Chornobyl

This branding project explores how we remember and imagine the Chornobyl Exclusion Zone. Our team developed a series of graphic marks representing each year since the nuclear disaster—visualising time as a spiral that transforms tragedy into hope. Each logo speaks to a future without forgetting the past.

The visual system was applied across campaigns, posters, and digital platforms, inviting viewers to consider what the zone might become in the years ahead. It’s a meditation on resilience, decay, and the possibility of renewal.
