Brave factory

Brave factory is a documentary project exploring the creative festival of the same name. Set in a repurposed industrial complex, the event brings together musicians, artists and makers for a celebration of art and community. Our visuals reflect the raw beauty of the location: textured backgrounds, industrial motifs and dynamic layouts.

The goal was to convey the DIY ethos of the festival while acknowledging its professional execution. The result is an identity that feels both gritty and polished, mirroring the spirit of Brave factory itself.
