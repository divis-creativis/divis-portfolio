Bud

Bud is an exploration of urban renewal. We were tasked with reimagining aging city spaces as vibrant cultural hubs. Through responsive design and environmental graphics, we created an identity that adapts to its surroundings: modular forms, bold typography and warm colours translate into murals, wayfinding and digital interfaces.

The project demonstrates how design can invigorate communities, linking history with present creativity. Buildings aren’t just structures; they’re canvases for human stories.
